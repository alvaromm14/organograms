Organograms

A diagram that shows the structure of an organisation and the relationships between the different people, departments, and jobs at different levels within that organisation

Organograms (also known as org charts) show the structure of organisations - in our case UK Government departments.

The gallery presents an overview of a number of departments. Click on a department to explore it in more detail.

After selecting a department you can click and drag to pan, scroll up/down to zoom. Use pinch and drag gestures on touchscreens. Hover over a circle for more information.

The data is open data from the Institute for Government and data.gov.uk. This is an open source project and contributions are welcome.
